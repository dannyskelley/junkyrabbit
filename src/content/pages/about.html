Our Story

Hard Work Is What We Do

Junky Rabbit Labor was built on a simple idea: Detroit Metro residents deserve fast, reliable, affordable labor help. Without the runaround. No bait-and-switch pricing. No no shows. Just a crew that shows up, works hard, and leaves every job site better than they found it.

We started by helping neighbors move furniture and haul junk on Nextdoor. Word spread fast. Today we handle everything from same day junk removal and full property cleanouts to moving, snow removal, yard cleanup, painting, and more. Across the entire Metro Detroit area.

  • Licensed and insured in the state of Michigan
  • Same day and weekend service available 7 days a week
  • Transparent, upfront pricing. No hidden fees ever
  • Cash, Venmo, Cashapp & Apple Pay accepted
  • Free estimates. Text us a photo for the fastest response
  • Hundreds of satisfied customers across Metro Detroit

Meet the Owner

Lunden. Founder & Lead

Lunden started Junky Rabbit Labor after seeing how hard it was to find reliable, affordable help in Detroit. Too many services were overpriced, unreliable, or both. He set out to change that.

"I built this business on the idea that people deserve a fair shake. Show up on time, do the work right, and charge what's fair. That's it.". Lunden, Owner

Lunden leads every job personally or with a handpicked crew he trusts. Every job reflects on the Junky Rabbit name, so every job gets full attention from start to finish.
